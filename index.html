About

We provide future of energy

ORGONE Limited is a proudly indigenous Nigerian company specializing in power backup and renewable energy solutions. With nearly a decade of hands-on experience, we have established ourselves as trusted partners in driving the transition to a cleaner, more sustainable energy future.
We design, integrate, and install tailored energy systems across the country from compact solar hybrid systems for homes, to mini-grid solutions for rural electrification, and large-scale industrial power backup systems. Our bespoke approach ensures every solution is customized to meet each client’s unique energy needs.
As key players in Nigeria’s renewable energy space, we are known for delivering reliable, high-performance systems backed by seasoned professionals and premium-grade components. At ORGONE Tecnergy, excellence, sustainability, and client satisfaction are the cornerstones of everything we do.

Steps of cooperation

How We work

01 / 06

Initial Consultation

We begin with a thorough needs assessment understanding your energy challenges, goals, and expectations through a one-on-one session or site visit.

02 / 06

Site Evaluation & Auditing

Our experts conduct a detailed technical audit of your location, measuring energy consumption, available resources, and space suitability for solar or backup systems.

03 / 06

System Design & Proposal

Using insights from the audit, we design a custom solution optimized for efficiency and cost-effectiveness. A clear proposal is presented, outlining timelines, components, and budget.

04 / 06

Procurement & Logistics

We source high-quality components from trusted global manufacturers and handle logistics to ensure timely delivery of all required equipment.

05 / 06

Installation & Integration

Our certified technicians carry out professional installation, integrating the system with existing infrastructure while ensuring compliance with safety and quality standards.

06 / 06

Testing, Handover & Support

After rigorous testing and commissioning, we hand over a fully operational system with training and documentation. Our after-sales support ensures long-term performance and peace of mind.

Kaduna Residential Solar Project

10-home solar hybrid solution

We provided full solar energy integration for a residential estate in Kaduna. Each home was equipped with a hybrid system combining solar panels, inverters, and battery storage. The project significantly reduced energy costs for residents while ensuring uninterrupted power even during grid outages. The system was tailored to household usage patterns and backed with remote monitoring and on-site support.
